Crystals

Crystals have facinated humans for thousands of years. They are not only used for decoration, many of the first radios were invented using crystals to transmit the radio waves. Some watches like Quartz watches use crystals to this day. They have always been viewed as a thing of beauty and are still often placed with diamonds or others stones inside peices of jewelry

Uses for Crystals

Quartz Crystals have a property called Pietzoelectricity which can generate and electric field, this causes them to be very useful in radio and vidio equipments. Silicon Crystals can be used in making chips the can power computers and the photovoltaic cells in solar technology. Crystals are also used for jewelry, decoration, meditation, and for healing purposes.
